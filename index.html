Rucoy Training Calculator

Input into the following fields the corresponding information to receive insights for your AFK train/Powertrain.

Overview:
This calculator will accurately indicate the best monster for you to train, the average training duration until the monster dies (keep in mind that monsters exhaust after 7 minutes 30 seconds), and the stats needed to progress to the next monster. Play around with different combinations of training weapons and equips to find what works best.

"Calculate for Powertrain" explained:
If this box is unchecked, the calculator will show results for training AFK. Checking the box will indicate to the calculator that you want to powertrain; if you are using magic, ensure to also select "Magic User".

Parameters explained:
"Base level" is the level that is seen above your character's head. "Stat Level" should be your actual stat level (without equips) that you intend to train. "Extra Equips" refers to the stat-boosting ring/necklace/armor that you will be wearing while training, all added together (negative value for void equips). "Attack of Weapon" is the strength of your training weapon, which can be found by reading its value from the inventory.

Advanced Options explained:
"Desired Efficiency" allows you to choose how efficient you want your training to be. Using AFK training as an example, choosing 90% efficiency will ensure that the calculator only shows monsters that you can train at 3,240 ticks per hour or better; the max value that you can select is 99%. "Enable Crit Ring" indicates to the calculator that you intend to use a crit ring while training.

Limitations:
This calculator unfortunately cannot indicate whether or not you are able to tank the resultant monster. Thus it is possible that the calculator may reccomend a monster that is too strong for you.
